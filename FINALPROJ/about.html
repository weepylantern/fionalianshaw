Fiona "Ailian" Shaw

Hi! I'm Fiona Shaw, an artist & illustrator based in Southern California. I strive to create intentional & meaningful designs and illustrations that effectively communicate human emotion/experience.

I practice & analyze storytelling through Dungeons & Dragons, creative writing, reading, and, of course, others' art. By finding the most personal and human stories around me, I can channel those emotions and lessons into my work to express a story that everyone can be moved by.
